\section{Motivation}
Consider a $n\times p$ matrix $\mathbf{X}$ with the covariance matrix $\mathbf{X'X}\sim \mathbf{W}(n,\boldsymbol{\Sigma})$ where $\mathbf{W}$ denotes the Wishart distribution. Then $\mathbf{X'X}$ can be decomposed as
$$
\mathbf{S} = \mathbf{X'X} = \mathbf{ULU}' = \sum l_j\mathbf{u}_j\mathbf{u}_j'
$$
